CHOCOLATE CAKE

Time:40 mins.

Yield: Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  1. Margarine: 175 gms
  2. Castor sugar: 88 gms
  3. Cake flour: 155 gms
  4. Cocoa powder: 20 gms
  5. Baking powder: 2.5 gms
  6. Eggs: 2 nos
  7. Egg yolk: 4 nos
  8. Dark Chocolate" 60 gms
  9. Egg white: 4 nos
  10. Castor sugar: 80 gms
  11. Raisins: 20 gms
  12. Dark rum: 15 gms

Method/Steps:

  1. Scale all the ingredients and soak the raisins in the dark rum.
  2. Cream the margarine on the working table with the slow addition of castor sugar.
  3. Mix the egg yolk with the whole white eggs and add it slowly into the above mixture, creaming it well.
  4. Melt the dark chocolate on a bain-marie.
  5. Once the margarine mixture is creamed well by taking in all the eggs then add in the melted chocolate, and the raisins steeped in rum. Mix the mixture well.
  6. Start whiskling the egg white with castor sugar and once they form peaks add them to the cake batter and fold in the sieved cake flour with cocoa powder and baking powder in it.
  7. Pour the batter into the cake moulds and bake them at 175° till done.
  8. Once aked remove it from the oven and serve as required.
